Makeup Work Requirements and Expectations

There may be times when makeup work is required to receive credit in seminary. Makeup work is usually given due to absences. Any unexcused absences require makeup work to be done to receive credit. Any absences below 75 percent attendance require makeup work to be done as well. Excessive tardiness may also cause makeup work to be needed.

When makeup work is needed I will give you the assignment that needs to be completed. The assignment will from the lesson(s) you missed. There may be times when a different assignment will be given (like for tardies). The lesson(s) and where to go to complete the assignment will be given. It is expected for you to go to the lesson(s) and complete what is asked. This usually consists of reading the missed scriptures, finding the the lesson(s), and writing down the answers to the questions you are asked. You can either turn in the paperwork or email it to me at nielsenkl@churchofjesuschrist.org
